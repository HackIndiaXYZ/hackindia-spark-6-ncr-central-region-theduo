How ScanBill Works

A simple 4-step flow that turns a normal payment into a useful business system.

01

Seller Selects Items

The seller quickly chooses the purchased items from a simple product list.

02

ScanBill automatically creates the exact payment amount as a ready-to-scan QR.

03

04

Inventory + Insights Update Automatically
